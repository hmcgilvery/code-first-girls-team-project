    Are you having trouble sleeping?

    Losing a night's sleep or even more can really afect our mind and energy levels the next day. Having a good sleep hygiene is as important as eating healthy and exercising. However, it can be a little difficult to fall asleep when we have so many distractions in the bedroom: television, tablets and phones. Whether you have regular or occcasional sleep issues you can learn how to better manage your sleep by introducing some new healthy habits into your daily routine.

    Six steps to a restful sleep

    1. Stick to a sleep schedule- try to go to sleep and wake up at the same time every day.
    2. If you have things on your mind, try to put them on paper.
    3. Avoid stimulants before bed time- alcohol, sugar and caffeine.
    4. Get outside every day to breathe fresh air.
    5. Exercise daily for a minimum of 30 minutes.
    6. Avoid taking your devices to bed and ensure you limit screen time 2 hours before bed.
