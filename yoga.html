    What is Yoga and what are the benefits?

    Yoga is a practice which connects the mind and the body through different poses, meditation and breathing techniques. It has a lot of benefits both for the mind and the body. Let's start with the mind. Yoga helps elevate your mood, improves your concentration, lowers stress and anxiety feelings and it decreases depression. Amongst some of the benefits for the body we can mention that Yoga stretches your muscles and improves your posture and core strength. When we coordinate movement with the breath and paying attention to the sensations in the body as we move, feeling the experience in that moment - Yoga becomes a mindful exercise. Train your mind and body to strenghten your mental and physical well-being by following the videos below.
