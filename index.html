    What is Mindfulness?

    Mindfulness is the idea of learning how to be present in the moment, aware of our thoughts and feelings without judgement or interpretation. Mindfulness uses techniques such as breathing methods and guided meditation to help relax the body and the mind. Breathing is something we do every day but not something we pay attention to. When we are more in tune with our breath, our mind and our body we develop skills which help us deal with daily stress.
    Take a moment and think about when was the last time you put your phone down and got rid of all of the distractions around you? The last time you stood still and let your mind relax? Mindful Moments introduces you to a variety of techniques which can benefit your life. Unhook from your thoughts and emotions and live a more happy and authentic life by practicing the below recommended techniques.
